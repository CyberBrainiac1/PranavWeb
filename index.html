Hey, I'm Pranav 👋 — a high-school student from San Jose, founder of the FTC robotics team Evergreen Dragons, and a core member of Hack Club where some of my best projects come to life.

I'm obsessed with hardware. I love designing intricate mechanisms in SolidWorks and Onshape, getting my hands dirty machining and 3D-printing parts, and integrating custom PCBs to bring systems to life. There's something uniquely satisfying about taking an idea from CAD and holding the finished result in your hand.

I've picked up Python, HTML, and CSS along the way, and I lean on AI to level up my coding and learning. Right now I'm deep in the weeds building a DIY robotic hand and experimenting with real-time image classification on embedded hardware—fun stuff that actually pushes my limits.

I thrive on rapid iteration, hands-on problem solving, and shipping things that work. I'm always excited to connect with mentors, fellow makers, and anyone building cool stuff. Also exploring Isaac Sim to bring AI and robotics together in a serious way.
