High-school robotics builder based in San Jose, CA.

Hey, I'm Pranav - a high-school student from San Jose, founder of the FTC robotics team Evergreen Dragons, and an active member of Hack Club where a lot of my projects take shape.

I spend most of my time building hardware: designing mechanisms in SolidWorks and Onshape, machining and 3D-printing parts, and integrating custom PCBs to bring systems to life. The best part is turning a CAD model into something you can actually hold.

Along the way I've picked up Python, HTML, and CSS, and I use AI to help me learn and debug. Lately I'm building a DIY robotic hand and experimenting with real-time image classification on embedded hardware.

I like rapid iteration, hands-on problem solving, and shipping things that work. I'm always up for connecting with mentors, fellow makers, and anyone building cool stuff. I'm also exploring Isaac Sim to bring AI and robotics together in a more serious way.
